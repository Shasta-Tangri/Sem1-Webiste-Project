Streaming: Don't Look Up

What would you do if an asteroid was heading for earth and full destruction was imminent? Streaming on Netflix December 24th, Leonardo DiCaprio and Jennifer Lawrence star in Don’t Look Up as two low-level astronomers who attempt to warn mankind about their enviable destruction via a media tour. Directed, written and, produced by Adam McKay – DiCaprio and Lawrence are joined by a fully star-studded cast including Meryl Streep, Jonah Hill, Tyler Perry, and Ariana Grande, and much more in this Netflix Holiday release.
